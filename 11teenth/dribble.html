Dribble Doctrine

Budget (Standard)

≤ 7 per match. World Cup verified. Each dribble is a spend. The budget forces quality.

Budget (Semi-final)

Open. Romance earns its moment. The biggest stage, the freest game.

Order of Operations

Pass → Carry → Dribble. Only dribble when the pass is worse and the carry is blocked.

Pausa Before Action

Every dribble begins with a pause — whether visible or internal. Read first. React never.

La Pausa (Iniesta Rule)

Freeze everything. Let the defender make the first move. The dribble is the response.

Probabilistic vs Romantic

Probabilistic dribblers always win in the end. The romantic dribble is real — it must be earned, not forced.
