Anti-Injury / Maximum Gain Protocol

≤ 7 dribbles per match (standard)
Open dribble limit in semi-finals
Ball moves faster than any runner
Position before possession
Recover before attacking
Never force flair — earn it
Discomfort is data, not failure
5-second rule: shape after turnover

⬡ Probabilistic

  • Maximum output, minimum touches
  • High % passes, compounded over 90min
  • Dribble budget strictly managed
  • Anticipation over reaction
  • Win small moments; scoreline follows
  • Probabilistic players always win in the end

♡ Romantic

  • Expressive, spontaneous, crowd-moving
  • High ceiling, high variance
  • Football's inherent beauty — never force it
  • Let it emerge from good probabilistic play
  • Valid, beautiful — not a tournament strategy
  • The game's inheritance, not its engine
