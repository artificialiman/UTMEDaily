📋 How to Use This Payment Management System

  1. Add Students: Click Add Student. Enter the student's full name, class, parent name, phone, and fee breakdown. The Total Fee is calculated automatically.
  2. Record Payments: Use the Record Payment button. Select the student, enter the amount paid, date, receipt number, method, and your name. Balance and Status update instantly.
  3. Track Defaulters: The Defaulters List tab automatically shows students who haven't paid in full.
  4. Class Summary: The Summary tab shows per-class aggregates — totals, collections, and rates.
  5. Backup Regularly: CSV backups are automatically saved after every change. Use the Backup & Restore tab to manually export or restore data.
