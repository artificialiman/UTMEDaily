🔹 Topic 1: Dividing by Fractions (Immediate Practice)
QUESTION 1
Simplify \( 8 \div \frac{2}{3} \).
(A) 12(B) \(\frac{16}{3}\)(C) 4(D) 24
Solution: \(8 \div \frac{2}{3} = 8 \times \frac{3}{2} = \frac{24}{2} = 12\).
Answer: A
QUESTION 2
Work out \( 15 \div \frac{5}{7} \).
(A) \(\frac{75}{7}\)(B) 21(C) 10(D) \(\frac{15}{35}\)
Solution: \(15 \div \frac{5}{7} = 15 \times \frac{7}{5} = \frac{105}{5} = 21\).
Answer: B
QUESTION 3
Evaluate \( 6 \div \frac{22}{7} \).
(A) \(\frac{132}{7}\)(B) \(\frac{21}{11}\)(C) \(\frac{42}{22}\)(D) \(\frac{21}{11}\)
Solution: \(6 \div \frac{22}{7} = 6 \times \frac{7}{22} = \frac{42}{22} = \frac{21}{11}\). So \(\frac{21}{11}\).
Answer: B (also D, but B is simplified)
QUESTION 4
A rope 12 m long is cut into pieces each \(\frac{3}{4}\) m long. How many pieces are obtained?
(A) 9(B) 16(C) 15(D) 8
Solution: Number of pieces = \(12 \div \frac{3}{4} = 12 \times \frac{4}{3} = 16\).
Answer: B

🔹 Topic 6: Area of Circles & Related Shapes (Immediate)

QUESTION 24
