The Three Portals — What Your School Gets
Portal 01
Teacher Portal
Each subject teacher logs in to their dedicated profile and enters CA and exam scores per student, per term. The interface is designed for speed: one page, one class, one subject at a time. Scores are saved and immediately reflected in the admin broadsheet. No spreadsheets emailed across the school. No data entry duplicated.

Portal 02
Admin Broadsheet
The administrative core of the system. An admin user selects any class — e.g. SS1 Science — and sees every student's score across every subject in one editable grid. CA, exam, and total are visible inline. The system calculates the sum of all subject totals, assigns grades, and generates a class position ranking. When the term is closed, the admin exports a CSV that feeds directly into individual student report sheets — printed or digital. No need to log into teacher profiles. No manual compilation.

Portal 03 — Revenue Generator
Student Result Checker
Parents and students access term results using a unique PIN printed on a physical result checker card — sold by the school each term. The portal authenticates the card PIN and displays the student's full result sheet. This creates a recurring termly revenue stream that directly offsets and eventually far exceeds the original $250 investment. Card pricing, design, and distribution are handled by the school; the system handles authentication and display.
